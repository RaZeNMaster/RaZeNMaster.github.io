About

I'm a developer from Germany who basically grew up surrounded by code — my whole family are developers, so I started early and have been programming for 6 years now.

What drives me is building things that actually work — and that genuinely help or enrich the people who use them. I love pushing an idea as far as it goes and discovering what's really possible to build. Along the way I care a lot about privacy-first and high-performance design.

Right now I'm building FTHRClips, a privacy-first game clipping tool written in C++, and contributing to LearnE, a safe AI knowledge app for kids. When I'm not coding, you'll usually find me gaming, at the gym, or spending time with my girlfriend — so much for the stereotype that developers are basement-dwelling nerds with no life. 😄

FAQ

What are you working on right now?
Mainly FTHRClips — a privacy-first, high-performance game clipping tool written in C++ with a PyQt6 UI — plus contributing to LearnE, a safe AI knowledge app for kids.
What's your main tech stack?
Day to day: TypeScript / JavaScript, React and Node on the web; Python, Java and Go on the backend; React Native & Expo for mobile; and C++ / Qt for performance-critical work.
Do you build websites or apps on request?
Yes! I build websites, web apps, mobile apps and bots/tools on request. Tell me what you need via email or Discord and we'll take it from there — see the Work with me section.
Are you open to collaborating?
Yes — I'm always happy to talk about interesting projects, especially anything around privacy, performance or building useful tools. Reach out via email or Discord.
Why "privacy-first"?
I think software should work for the user, not harvest them. Wherever it makes sense, I build with no tracking, no accounts and no unnecessary data collection.
How can I reach you?
Email me at weissheimer.tom@gmail.com, message me on Discord, or find me on GitHub as @RaZeNMaster.
